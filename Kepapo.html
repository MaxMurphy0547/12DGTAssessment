About Kepapo Cabin

The Kepapo Cabin is best for familys, large groups of friends, or even with strangers. it offers a comfortable bedroom that holds up to 40 people, the cabin is placed right on the mountain providing on the go access to 550 hectares of skiing right from the doorstep.

Enjoy amazing views of the surrounding mountain, a large living area, and everything you need after a long day of hitting the slopes

Mountain Access

The Kepapo cabin sits on New Zealands most beutiful ski field, providing access to the best skiing youll ever epxerience. wether your a beginner or an expert, this cabin allows access to ski fields youll never forget
